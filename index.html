ABOUT THIS PAGE

This page is about my view of dad jokes.

I show them through an early-internet pop-up advertisement style because many people think dad jokes are random, cheesy, repetitive, and sometimes nonsense.

I wanted to challenge that idea and make them fun (because dad jokes are FUN(at least some of them)).

I designed the screen to feel intentionally chaotic so users can experience an overload of dad jokes lingering in their minds, but laugh at the same time.

HOW IT WORKS

  • Drag tabs by the blue title bar.
  • + expands a tab and reveals more content.
  • × closes a tab.
  • I challenge you to delete all tabs.
